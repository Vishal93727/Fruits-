COFFEE

The aim of this case study is to design and analyze a rain alarm circuit and explore its practical applications in real-life scenarios, particularly in agriculture. The primary objective is to demonstrate how this circuit can be used to detect rainfall early, alert users to take preventive actions, and protect crops, equipment, or other outdoor assets from rain-induced damage. Additionally, the study aims to highlight the benefits, challenges, and potential improvements of using such a simple, cost-effective technology in farming operations.
